How we work

Our student projects consist of:

1-WEEK DESIGN SPRINT

A workshop to identify an achievable technical solution, followed by close collaboration to create user-centered designs that are functional and simple to use.

2-WEEK BUILD SPRINT

A team of student developers will build you a web app MVP (minimum viable product) that allows you to prototype your solution.



After the free MVP, you can discuss with us options to develop your project further.

We host 3 rounds of Student Projects each year - the next will begin in mid-May 2018 and we are currently in conversation with potential partners. If your organisation has a problem or challenge that could be solved by tech, please get in touch!
